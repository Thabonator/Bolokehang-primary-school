OUR RESULTS

Bolokehang Intermediate School has achieved an 90% pass rate, despite challenges we faced in the year. In addition the foundation phase achieved 100% pass rate for Grade R and 94% from Grade 1-3.Our intermediate phase grades(4,5 and 6) achieved 93% pass rate,Lastly our grade 7 senior phase managed to obtain 86%. These are amazing results where obtained during a pandemic. Well done to every learner who contributed to these results with their hard work, dedication and perseverance. We would also like to thank our staff for their unwavering commitment and support to the learners throughout 2020. The management team is proud of all of you and beyond thankful to kick off 2021 on such a positive note.
